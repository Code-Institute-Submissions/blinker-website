What We Listen to

At Blinker we do not like the atmosphere to be painted on the wall so our eclectic taste in music is always changing. We like to read the room and see how you are feeling and match your moods with music that suits or failing that lift your mood with music. We have resident DJ's that play regularly and often share their time slots with local DJ's who are new to the music scene and trying to get their music heard. We also have an in house disco every 2 months hosted by our very own promoter Miss Tangerine. We believe our music caters to everyone and we try to keep a close eye to the music scene and a close ear to the street so we can ever change our style and try to bring you the newest local talent.

Regal Disco

Our very own Miss Tangerine throws the liveliest of discos at Blinker bar. Always a vibrant and intoxicating experience, Regal Disco brings you the best of carefully selected Afrobeat, cumbia, global bass and tropical sounds from around the globe – so boogying lunacy is sure to ensue.

After the success of last months party, we are excited to be inviting Regal Disco back to curate the after-party Blinker bar. Regal Disco wouldn’t be the same without their magnificent resident DJs Long Guy ‘Out of Quiet’ AKA L BOOGIE and Sunny Wray (both resident DJ's at Blinker).

Out Of Quiet alias of DJ, Lowell Walbank, whose weekly excursions on Reform Radio celebrate sounds from around the world and across the spectrum. After years DJing together as part of the Mind On Fire Collective, they have been working together but not alongside each other at Blinker. he’s reunited once again with fellow Mind On Fire selector Sunny Wray, who will be bringing us some Caliente beats from his recent excursion to Barcelona.

Miss Puerto Rico is bringing you some Latina fire! You know her from her amazing Reform Radio show and she’s been behind the decks with us before and at last year’s SFTOC. She knows what makes you wanna move your body all night and she has killer fan skills!

Also playing the sexy duo King Bongo, Djs Bingo Bingo and Bobbyganoush. Bobbyganoush is a massive music passionate and will know how to make you wanna shake what you’ve got.

CONCEPT: Tropical dream catcher all over the club and balloons as well as confetti guns and glitter face paint.

Resident DJ's

Dividing the week between them they are always on hand to make sure the atmosphere is just right.
