Who We Are?

Blinker is a cocktail bar that is dedicated to good times, good times for the patrons, good times for the staff and good times for the planet. We focus on making deliciously sustainable drinks that do not compromise flavour in our bid to make the world more sustainable. Our mission is to be a community orientated venue that does our bit for making our community a better place. We try to source the most local ingredients for our cocktails and for our soon to be opened kitchen. We try to source all our beers from the most local breweries and our spirits from the most local distilleries. We are conscious about our products and even though some of our wines are shipped from the continent we take steps to ensure they have been grown sustainably and have a small carbon footprint. We source local talent to set up our music events and offer a platform for emerging DJ’s and musicians through our “locally live” music night every Thursday.
