What We Drink

At Blinker we believe in sustainability and local produce, so we try to source all of our sauce from the closest source possible. Our bartenders all have a love for alcohol and sustainability and thrive on the challenge of making drinks of outstanding quality with sustainable ingredients. We look at all factors in the selection of our refreshment’s quality is first and foremost as we want all of our patrons to have the best possible experience and deliver them with the highest quality that we can offer. Followed by this is sustainability this means we genuinely look at all factors and facets of what we serve. From simply using seasonable fruits o checking the road and air mileage of our products we do everything we can to reduce our carbon footprint. Last but not least we want our menus to be fun. Our cocktails to be creative and daring, the wines we use to be innovative and whilst still elegant and our beers to be rotated at the speed of the ever-booming industry. We hope you enjoy drinking our drinks as much as we enjoyed curating this menu.

Cocktails

Our cocktail menu is seasonably changing affair. We follow he seasons so that our ingredients are fresh and can be locally sourced. We also try and re use every bit of everything we use enabling our bar team to get creative and experimental. As stated earlier we quality is our number one goal and we will not compromise the taste for anything…..we will simply find a new way of doing it.

Blinker Martini

Ice cold Gin or Vodka with dry vermouth and olives or a twist of in season citrus.

Blinker Manhattan

Whiskey, sweet vermouth bitters and our own brandy soaked cherries.

Blinker Sazerac

Rye whiskey with Cognac, sugar, Absinthe and a seasonal citrus twist.

Sunshne Sling

Freshly squeezed apricot juice with lime, in house grown thyme and a locally sourced Gin.

The Rick Roll

Recycled red wine with spiced syrup and rum.

Almond Margarita

In house made almond orgeat, lime and Tequila.

Wine

Every winery we partner with, we have a special relationship with. We try to send members of our team to their vineyards and get them to stay with us. Ha way we can make sure hay they are doing everything they can to be ethical and sustainable and they can make sure that we have the right environment for them to showcase their wine in.
