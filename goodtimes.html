How You Book

Blinker is a busy venue boasting a downstairs club venue as well as an upstairs rooftop cocktail garden bar. We have a high turnover of patrons so to ensure you get a table we will take reservations for groups of 10 or more. If you are after any of the large party packages or you are simply a large group and want to ensure a place to sit please fill out our booking form below, letting us know the date you wish to book and a rough idea of what you have in mind. We will do our best to accommodate you and your party. It may take up to 24 hours but one of our events team will get back to you to finalise the details.
