How You Book

Blinker is a busy venue boasting a downstairs club venue as-well as an upstairs rooftop cocktail garden bar We do see a high turnover of patrons so to ensure your you can get a booking we do take reservations for groups of 10 or more. So, if you are after any of large party packages or simply are in a large group and wan to ensure a place to sit please fill out are booking form below, letting us know the date you wish to book and rough idea of what you have in mind we will do our best to accommodate you and your party. It may take up to 24 hours but one of events team will get back to you to finalise the details.
